üìö Essential Context: 1898-1904

üá∫üá∏ Spanish-American War (1898)

üáµüá≠ Philippine-American War (1899-1902)

üìú Constitutional Lens: Empire vs. Republic

‚öñÔ∏è Anti-Imperial Constitutional Arguments
