‚öñÔ∏è Why Balance is Required

Conservation of Mass

Atoms cannot be created or destroyed - only rearranged

üî¨ Scientific Law

  • Atoms are not created in reactions
  • Atoms are not destroyed
  • They only change partners
  • Total mass stays the same

üßÆ Mathematical Rule

  • Same number of each atom type
  • Must be equal on both sides
  • Like balancing a scale
  • Required for equation to be correct
