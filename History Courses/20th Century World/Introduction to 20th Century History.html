Historical Thinking vs. Memorization

Traditional Approach

  • Memorize: "WWI started in 1914"
  • Accept: "This is what happened"
  • Goal: Remember facts for tests

Historical Thinking

  • Question: "Why did this war start when it did?"
  • Investigate: "Who says this happened, and why?"
  • Goal: Think like a detective

In this course, you'll develop the skills to analyze evidence, question sources, and understand multiple perspectives on historical events.

From Past to Present

The five forces we've identified continue to shape our world today:

  • Economic relationships between developed and developing nations
  • Technological advancement in AI, genetics, and climate science
  • Political competition between democratic and authoritarian systems
  • Independence movements and struggles for self-determination
  • International organizations working on global challenges
