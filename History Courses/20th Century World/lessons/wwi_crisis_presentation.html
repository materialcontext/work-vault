The Peace to End All Peace?

Wilson's 14 Points

  • Open diplomacy
  • Freedom of the seas
  • Free trade
  • Self-determination
  • League of Nations
  • "Peace without victory"

Allied Demands

  • German war guilt
  • Massive reparations
  • Military restrictions
  • Territorial losses
  • No German League membership
  • "Make Germany pay"
Major Terms of the Treaty of Versailles
  • Article 231 (War Guilt): Germany accepts full responsibility for the war
  • Reparations: Germany must pay for all civilian damage
  • Military: Army limited to 100,000 men, no tanks, planes, or submarines
  • Rhineland: Demilitarized buffer zone
  • Territorial: Alsace-Lorraine to France, Polish Corridor created
  • Colonies: All German colonies redistributed as League mandates
  • League: Created but Germany initially excluded
  • Enforcement: Allied occupation of German territory until compliance
