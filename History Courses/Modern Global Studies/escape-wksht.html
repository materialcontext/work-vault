Historical Source Analysis Worksheet

Systematic Investigation of Primary Sources

E
Evidence
What specific information does this source provide?
S
Source
Who created this and what type of source is it?
C
Context
What historical circumstances surrounded its creation?
A
Audience
Who was the intended audience?
P
Purpose
Why was this source created?
E
Evaluation
How reliable and useful is this source?
Remember: Good historical analysis considers multiple perspectives and uses evidence to support conclusions.
