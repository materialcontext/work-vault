üîÑ Iranian Cultural Adaptation Pattern

The Secret of Iranian Survival

How do you maintain your identity when more powerful forces conquer you?

Step 1
Accept new system - Don't resist directly when you're too weak
Step 2
Excel within it - Become indispensable to the new rulers
Step 3
Influence from within - Shape the system with your values
Step 4
Create synthesis - Make it distinctively Iranian

With Mongols (13th c.): Persians convert Mongol rulers to Islam
With Western influence (20th c.): Iranians try to modernize on their own terms
With 1979 Revolution: Create "Islamic Republic" - neither purely traditional nor Western
